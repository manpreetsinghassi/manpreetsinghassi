Nexum

Overview

Nexum is a platform designed to connect post-secondary students and alumni, providing valuable networking opportunities to help students succeed in their job search. By collecting data on graduates, such as their employment status, companies they work for, and other relevant information, post-secondary institutions can use Nexum to promote their programs and demonstrate the success of their alumni. When a post-secondary institution offers the Nexum platform to its students, it signals a commitment to their success and provides access to a vast network of profiles from others who have completed the same program, creating new possibilities for future career growth.

Problem

For many students, finding a job in their field after completing their studies is a top concern. Before choosing a post-secondary program, students conduct extensive research on institutions to ensure that they will receive a valuable education that leads to meaningful employment opportunities. Critical questions include: Will I be able to find a job after completing this program? How many individuals have graduated from this program, and how many are currently working in the industry? These questions are paramount for students, who not only invest their financial resources in their education, but also their valuable time and effort. To help make this decision easier and support students in their job search, we created Nexum. This powerful platform connects post-secondary students and alumni, providing a vast network of resources and opportunities to help them succeed in their chosen field. By leveraging data and insights on graduates, Nexum helps students make informed decisions about their post-secondary education and achieve their professional goals with greater ease.

Solution

Manage students and alumni.

Our SaaS application allows institution adminis- trators to track and manage students and alumni. Admins are provided an intuitive way to access information in list and graphical formats. The UI is focused on providing simplicity and functional behavior.

Connection building among students and alumni.

Enhancing communication between students and alumni is the core of our application. Everything we have built revolves around im- proving the interaction of students with other students, alumni and job opportunities that are shared within a private community of the institution.

Find and share jobs.

Our application tries to make it easier for students by sharing exclusive community job oppportunities that are visible to the students and alumnis of the instituition. These job listings make it easier and more comfortable for students as they try to find a job in their industry.

Logo

×

After much consideration, our team decided to go for a logo based on a typeface. Every designer in our team presented 3 potential logos based on a rough sketch and we all agreed this logo to be part of our brand identity. It is a custom Serif typeface that has been curved more on the edges to make it stand out and to create a stylish look for our product “Stylify” which means “style in life”.

Behind the typeface, we have created a combination of lines that depicts the mirrors in salons. Mirrors carry great importance in hair salons as their main function is to show the customer how the stylists are doing their job and they can guide stylists to their desired results.

We wanted to create a logo that is unique so first-time users could get some idea about what our product is all about before using it.

Color System

×

Our reason behind choosing this color palette is that we wanted to stand out from our competition. It is an application specifically for salons and beauty-related businesses. We wanted to create the same kind of atmosphere here in our design as their businesses and after considering a lot of other color combinations, this is our final color palette which I believe works well with our design choice inspired by neo-brutalism and neo-pop. This color palette suits our bold choice of design and we have two functional colors for success and error in input fields. This color choice passes all the contrast checkers and makes us different from our competitors.

Typography

×

To fit our bold design we wanted to use a combination of Playfair Display(Serif) and Figtree(Sans-serif) typefaces. Serif font is used to match the style and classiness of our application and sans-serif font is for good readability for the user. I believe these typefaces match our application perfectly because we needed something with more font families for different parts of our application and we didn’t want to have more than 2 fonts as it might be overwhelming for the user.
